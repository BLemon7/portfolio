About


My name is Brendan Lemon. I am currently a first year Interaction Design student at Belfast School of Art. I enrolled in the course with little knowledge of graphic or web design, coming here from an Architecture based course. I haven't regretted this decision and think I have adapted quite well, and I'm learning more everyday.

I have developed a particular taste for icon design and, mainly, UXD so far. We'll see how far I can run with it.

Away from my Macbook you can find me on the mats training Brazilian Jiu Jitsu at Gracie Barra, Belfast.

Monogram

Below you can see the monogram I designed based on my initials - BL. The overall shape is based on basic geometric shapes for a contemporary look. The colour scheme utilises purple to convey the mood of creativity and elegance on the first impression. I have created an all-black version for use against contrasting backgrounds. The two versions can be seen below.

Word mark

Here is my word mark, which hasn't abstracted far from my monogram. I have added horizontal text as I think it is very effective for letterheads and website headers. The font choice is Exo which I have incorporated into my brand identity. I feel the sans type is effective at maintaining the contemporary style which I have employed in my monogram.

As with the monogram, I have created an all-black version for use against contrasting backgrounds. The two versions can be seen below.

Visual Marque

My visual mark is based upon a unique part of me - my surname! You may question why this particular lemon is purple. A regular, yellow lemon would clash with my identity's purple colour scheme, so my solution was to make this visual mark even more eye catching by maintaining the brand identity.

Travel app concept

For IXD104 one of my projects was to create an app concept based on travelling. In a previous Master and Apprentice task (seen further down the page) I recreated a graphic of the Eiffel Tower. I enjoyed this so I created some other landmarks based on cities that interest me.

Infographic

The final task of IXD104 required me to create an infographic based on population. After brief research and some brainstorming, I decided to create an infographic based on the Black Death's effects on medieval Europe.

Master & Apprentice tasks

Here are some Master and Apprentice tasks I was given in IXD104. Images were provided and I had to replicate them as close as I could. I found this to be a useful way of learning and understanding the process of creating such icons and graphics which would have intimidated me when I first glanced at them!
